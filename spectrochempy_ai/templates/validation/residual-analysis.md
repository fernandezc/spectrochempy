# Residual Analysis

**template_id:** `residual_analysis`
**version:** `0.1.0`
**compatible_registry:** `0.1`

---

## Scientific Objective

Diagnose the quality of a fitted calibration or resolution model by
analysing the spectral residuals — the part of the data not captured
by the model. Large residuals, structured residuals (non-random
patterns), or observations with high Q residuals or Hotelling's T²
indicate that the model does not capture all systematic variation.
