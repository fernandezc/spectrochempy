**template_id:** `simplisma`
**version:** `0.1.0`
**compatible_registry:** `0.1`

---

## Scientific Objective

Identify pure variables (spectral channels where only one component
contributes) by analysing the relative standard deviation across
spectra. SIMPLISMA (SIMPLe-to-use Interactive Self-modelling Mixture
Analysis) iteratively selects variables that are both pure and
maximally dissimilar from previously selected ones. The resulting
pure-variable spectra serve as an initial spectral estimate for MCR-ALS.
